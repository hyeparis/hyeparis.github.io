ABOUT

Buddies is a mobile application which is developed in android studio in java. Buddies is the application which helps you to discover the things you love to do by organizing online groups with similar interests. You can host your own events and groups on your favorite topic and use a chat function to connect with the people in your group. Find people who have simliar tastes around you!
